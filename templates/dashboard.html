Why pmonitor.com

We on the pmonitor.com are eager to help you to monitor patients that need care 24X7 .This site allows doctors to monitor patients in varoius ways that is generally work of a nurse. By this nurse gets some extra tiime too and can help with other patients in the hospital.

Movement

This function allows the camera to capture the movement of the patient and report it their doctor. Only to be used for patients not expected to move(if they have been given a dose of morphine or are in coma). To be used in a position in which the patient is visible as it captures all movements.

Eye detection

A function specifically used for eyes only. This function allows the camera to see the eyes and report to the doctor accordingly. The function can tell if the patient is drowsy, sleeping or awake . To used directly above patinet's eye or from a position where the eyes are clearly visible/

Body movement

This function allows the camera to know if the patient has left the bed or not and act accordingly. To be used for patients that usually roams without permission(like in parkinsons). To be used from a position where the bed is clearly visible
